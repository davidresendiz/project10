About Page Download Dilemma

The dilemma I have chosen involves people illegally downloading music over the internet. I choose to download some music over the internet once in a while because listening to music from Youtube uses 2.5-6MB per minute and legally purchasing music can be expensive.
I download music off the internet because:

  • It's free
  • Incredibly convenient
  • Purchasing legal copies can be expensive
The problem with downloading music over the internet is:
  • A virus could be downloaded
  • It is copyright infringement and the fines can go up to $250,000, if caught
  • Spyware could be downloaded

In conclusion, I will not download music illegally over the internet as often as I used to because of the potential problems it could create.
